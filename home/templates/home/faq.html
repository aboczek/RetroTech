Frequently asked questions?

Delivery is done by third party companies like DHL, DPD or AnPost.
Shipping is done the same day if product was bought before 12pm, unless there is some delays which we will notify you about.
Tracking will be sent to you when we hand the parcel to delivery company.

Sell to us you need to contact us by **Sell to us** in navigation bar, fill in the details upload the pictures. We will contact you back and give you details where to send it to, when we receive the item we inspect the item, and contact you back with more details about quality, and money we offer you.

Returns are only accepted, if the item wasnt opened from original packaging or has been faulty from shipping. You have up to **60** days to return the product.

As a customer your product has 2 years warranty for NEW products, and for used also called second hand it all depends of grade how much it is worn out. For example Mint condition will have 6 months warranty, for example battle scared would give you 1 up to 2 months.
